Green Mean Juice

Ingredients

  • 1 small cucumber
  • 3-4 stalks of celery
  • 2 medium green (Granny Smith) apples
  • 10-15 spinach leaves
  • 3-4 sprigs of parsley
  • 1 inch piece of ginger
  • 1/2 a lemon
  • 1/2 a lime
  • 2-3 sprigs of basil, mint, and cilantro

Directions

  1. For best results use a slow masticating juicer
  2. Makes 1 serving of 16-20 oz (500-600 ml)
  3. Wash all the produce well
  4. Peel the lime and lemon
  5. Cut the fruits and vegetables into pieces for easy juicing
  6. Start with the cucumber and end with the apples
  7. Strain to remove pulp if desired
  8. Serve over ice for enhanced flavor
  9. Enjoy!
